  • Course Contents: Matrix Operations, Determinants, Solving Systems of Linear Equations, Vector Spaces, Subspaces, Bases, Linear Independence, Eigenvalues, Eigenvectors, Diagonalization of Matrices, Linear Transformations.

This course covers matrix theory and linear algebra, emphasizing topics useful in other disciplines. Due to its broad range of applications, linear algebra is one of the most widely taught subjects in college-level mathematics.
