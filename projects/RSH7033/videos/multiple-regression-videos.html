Multiple Regression Model

This curated video sequence supports Week 4 learning objectives (LO1–LO4) by introducing the theory and interpretation of multiple regression models. Students will learn how regression models extend from a single predictor to multiple predictors, how coefficients are interpreted, and how regression models are evaluated in applied statistical analysis.

Curated Video Sequence (~30–40 minutes total)

  • Video 1 – Introduction to Multiple Regression
  • Video 2 – Multiple Regression Model Structure
  • Video 3 – Interpreting Regression Coefficients
  • Video 4 – Model Evaluation and Diagnostics
  • Video 5 – Applications of Multiple Regression

Students should watch the videos in sequence and consider how multiple predictors improve model performance and enable more accurate statistical explanations of complex datasets.

These concepts form the foundation for many multivariate techniques, including MANOVA, discriminant analysis, and multivariate regression models.
